Alec is my name, Coding is my game

You can call me Akri, or you can know me as Kudatu. Akri is short for Akrivos/Akribos, a greek word (ακριβός) for dear. Kudatu is a word play of two words coming from two languages I speak — Code and Datu.

Welcome to my space, and as you come to know me in the digital world, may I leave the impression of love for people and coding.
